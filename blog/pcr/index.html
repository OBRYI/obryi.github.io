(im)perfection

short story of creation

Imperfect, what do you think about when you consider this word? I think about human being, sometimes, myself come up first. This artwork is not about human being, but at the same time it’s about us.

The idea behind this artwork was growing for a while now. First concept was born last year. I thought, what if we change viewport of 3d scene for real-world picture , and would be able to experience it the same way we experience interface of 3d software. I didn’t succeed, I implemented core of this concept but it was limited, because my program didn’t even make use of any photogrammetry algorithms which allows to scan environment with just photos. What I did instead is aligned photos manually, it was interesting but not at all what I expected.

Recently, inspiration hit and I did the “(im)perfection”, it also doesn’t make use of complicated algorithms. Instead, it does somewhat translates experience of 3d software, this time it wasn’t ideal either, but I was satisfied.

ok, but what it is about?

It’s about how our plans and vision of life are not quite the same as how it turns out to be in life. Meshes of figures are symbol for how we direct our life, e.g. plans for tomorrow, big and small dreams etc. Sketches are for how our plans and dreams come out to be if they ever do. Large white space around focus of the artwork is a symbol for forgotten/unfulfilled plans and dreams. It’s simple, yet, there’s another important component to it. The rough rotation is for unclear vision that we have it is for biases that we deal with when involved in processes of dreaming and making these dreams come true.
